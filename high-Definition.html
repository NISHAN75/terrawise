Scanners

Built for accuracy and reliability, these Leica scanners fit the needs of any project.

The Leica RTC360 is a lightweight, one-button 3D laser scanner that enables users to precisely document their projects in 3D. As a portable option, this laser is ready for hassle-free scanning on any job.

2D is a solution that provides automatic control of both slope and elevation. Simply put your blade on control, match your laser receivers to your laser beam, and you are ready to go.
