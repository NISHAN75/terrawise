Stephen Bridges

President

Stephen was Terrawise first employee over 20 years ago.  Starting as a service technician and rapidly developing into a service management role.  He developed our Machine Control division from the ground up, which has been often described as the industry standard by Leica-Geosystems.  In 2012 he became Vice President and Co-Owner of the business.  In 2021 he became the President of Terrawise Solutions.

Hobbies:  He loves spending time at the lake with his amazing wife Whitney and their two boys.

“Our business is not about the tangible things. It’s built on the dedication to our customer’s success, our relentless approach to solving their problems, and our belief of becoming an extension of their company. When we take care of them, they take care of us.”
